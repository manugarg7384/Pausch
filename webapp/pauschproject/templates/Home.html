Rules

Turn all the lights on!

1. Clicking on a block toggles the block and it's neighbors on or off
2. Continue toggling blocks until all the lights have been turned on

About

Lights On! is an interactive game by Hunter Modisett, Leslie Bloomfield, Youzi Wu, Felipe Vargas, Jeffrey Zhang, Manu Garg, and Kevin Huang. It was designed and built for the Spring 2015 mini-course Interaction & Expression Using the Pausch Bridge Lighting at Carnegie Mellon University.

Gameplay is controlled through this web app and impacts the visual output of the bridge LED panels in real-time.
